Senior Front End Developer

Job Description

We are looking for a Front-End Developer who is motivated to combine the art of design with the art of programming. You will work with the UI/UX designer and HTML/CSS team and bridge the gap between graphical design and technical implementation, taking an active role on both sides and defining how the application looks as well as how it works.

Required Skills
  • very strong background in JavaScript, jQuery, HTML5 and CSS3
  • strong expertise with Backbone.js & Marionette or other popular framework
  • deep understanding of Front End Security
  • experience with Ruby on Rails and in developing the frontend part for Ruby applications
  • experience with Twitter Bootstrap
  • knowledge of responsive design principles
  • fluent with git command line and able to work efficiently with Vagrant and Docker
Bonus Skills
  • experience with Jira and Agile software development
  • experience working with service-oriented architectures
  • experience working in a startup environment
  • interest in Bitcoin and FinTech
